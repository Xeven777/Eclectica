About US

Team Eclectica, the literary committee of Techno Main Salt Lake, is driven by students and guided by experienced faculty members. We aim to build a vibrant community that embraces literature through seminars, webinars, competitions, and workshops. Our mission is to nurture artistic expression, debates, drama, and quizzes in society. Through our active online presence on our blog, students can showcase their original content to the world. Join us to explore new avenues of creativity and passion throughout the year.
